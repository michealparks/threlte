A gizmo for visualizing global axes and to allow snap-to camera controls. The gizmo is rendered on top of the scene using a [`<HUD>`](/docs/reference/extras/hud) and controls the camera when clicked.

<Tip type="warning">
  Clicking to control the camera is only supported with `<OrbitControls>`. Other orbit control libraries, such as `camera-controls` are not yet supported.
</Tip>
